<p>My usual way of brewing coffee is to grind the coffee very fine, between espresso and turkish grind, and pour in boiling water.  In about two minutes, I stir; two minutes later I stir again. I added the coffee and water, pressurized with one nitrous oxide canister, and shook it all up for about one minute.
